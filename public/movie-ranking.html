Best Movies According To Me

My top 3 Movies of all time

Bumblebee

"Bumblebee" is a heartwarming film that beautifully captures the touching bond between a girl searching for her place in the world and a gentle, misunderstood robot from another universe.

Doctor Strange

"Doctor Strange" is a visually stunning and mystical Marvel film that follows the journey of a brilliant but arrogant neurosurgeon who, after a career-ending car accident, discovers the hidden world of magic and alternate dimensions.

Soul

"Soul" is an emotionally profound Pixar film that explores the journey of a middle school music teacher who, after an unexpected accident, discovers the true meaning of life and the importance of cherishing every moment.

Jumanji
